Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the food recognition?

+

Our AI can recognize thousands of food items with over 95% accuracy. For best results, take clear photos in good lighting with minimal background clutter.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Yes! We take privacy seriously. Your photos are processed securely and aren't stored permanently unless you explicitly choose to save them in your account.

Can I use NutriSnap offline?

+

NutriSnap requires an internet connection to analyze photos as our AI processing happens in the cloud for maximum accuracy.

How does NutriSnap work?

+

NutriSnap uses computer vision and machine learning to identify food items in your photos. Once identified, we reference our extensive nutrition database to provide detailed nutritional information.
