About NutriSnap

Our Mission

At NutriSnap, we believe that understanding what's in your food should be simple, accessible, and empowering. Our mission is to help everyone make informed dietary decisions by providing instant, accurate nutritional analysis through our cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Story

NutriSnap was founded in 2025 by a team of nutrition experts, data scientists, and tech enthusiasts who shared a common frustration: the difficulty of tracking nutrition accurately without spending hours logging meals manually.

What started as a passion project quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform that leverages machine learning to identify food items from images and provide detailed nutritional breakdowns in seconds.

Our Technology

We've trained our AI on millions of food images and nutritional data points to create one of the most accurate food recognition systems available. Our technology can identify thousands of food items, estimate portion sizes, and provide detailed macro and micronutrient information.

But we don't stop at analysis - NutriSnap also provides personalized recommendations based on your dietary goals and preferences, helping you make better choices every day.
